Weekly Critics

Our Memories

"I can practically smell this place! There’s nothing like dropping a coin and hearing that first 'round' call. Modern games might look amazing, but competing shoulder-to-shoulder on Street Fighter hits different. I really missed this vibe; for me, playing retro isn't just about gaming, it’s pure therapy."

"My dad’s excited laugh, my mom’s focused gaze, and my brother and I trying to beat Super Mario... This moment gathered around the CRT glow is the heart of our 80s family life. Retro gaming wasn't just fun for us; it was a priceless bond. What a warm, golden era!"

"My brother’s intense focus and my pure joy right beside him... That 1990 evening spent chasing Pac-Man through mazes feels like yesterday. These moments, sharing joysticks shoulder-to-shoulder, weren't just about gaming; they were the memories that bonded us. Playing retro is truly the most heartfelt story of our childhood."

"Back in '97, in my favorite corner of my room with the Game Boy that meant the world to me... Mario on the CRT TV and Kurt Cobain on the wall; life was simple and peak happiness. This photo isn't just a memory; it's where my gaming passion truly began. What a time to be alive!"
